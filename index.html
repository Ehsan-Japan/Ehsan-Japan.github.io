About

I am Ehsan Alizadeh, a master student at Osaka University, Japan. I am advised by Professor Takafumi Fujita and Professor Akira Oiwa.

Research Interest:

My research aims to find new methods of automatically tuning semiconductor spin qubits into desirable by using machine learning approaches.

I utilize concepts and tools in image processing and computer vision to formalize new autotuning methods and explore new questions spawning from the following directions:

  • How can we automatically form a triple quantum dot in gate-based quantum dot arrays ?
  • How can we automatically extract vitual gates in the Ray-Based Classification framework ?
