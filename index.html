About

I am Ehsan Alizadeh, a master student at Osaka University, Japan. I am advised by Professor Takafumi Fujita and Professor Akira Oiwa.

Research Interest:

My research aims to find new methods of automatically tuning semiconductor spin qubits into desirable states by using machine learning approaches.

I utilize concepts and tools in image processing and computer vision to formalize new autotuning methods and explore new questions spawning from the following directions:

  • When can quantum machines learn and predict better than classical machines?
  • How to automate the qubit?
