About

I am a second-year physics master student in the Department of Quantum System Electronics at Osaka University, Japan. I do research on automated tuning of gate-defined quantum dots. I am advised by Dr. Takafumi Fujita and Professor Akira Oiwa.

Projects

Automated Spin Qubit Initialization

Developed a machine learning algorithm to automate the initialization of spin qubits, improving efficiency and accuracy in quantum computing experiments.

Quantum Dot Tuning Automation

Created a computer vision system to automatically tune quantum dots for optimal performance, reducing manual intervention and error rates.

Spin Qubit Readout Optimization

Implemented a data-driven approach to optimize the readout process of spin qubits, enhancing the reliability and speed of quantum measurements.
